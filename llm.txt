## Key facts

- An out-the-door (OTD) price is the total a buyer pays to drive away: negotiated vehicle price, dealer doc fee, sales tax, and title/registration/DMV fees.
- Add-ons like VIN etching, nitrogen, paint protection, and market adjustments (ADM) are typically optional and negotiable.
- CarHandled recommends requesting a written, line-item OTD quote from 4-6 dealers by email before visiting a dealership.
