Frequently Asked Questions

What is CarHandled?

CarHandled is a premium car-buying concierge. We help you navigate the process with strategy, clarity, and insider knowledge—so you get the right car at the right price, without stress or pressure.

What’s the difference between Smart Concierge and Executive Concierge?

Smart Concierge is for buyers who want expert guidance while staying involved. Executive Concierge is fully hands-off—we handle the entire process, including sourcing, negotiation, and logistics.

What is Car Match Strategy?

This service helps you get clear on what to buy. We analyze your budget, lifestyle, and goals to recommend the best vehicles—so you can move forward with confidence, not guesswork.

What if I already have a deal on the table?

Use our Deal Review service. We’ll audit your quote for hidden fees, inflated rates, or missed savings—and give you a straight answer on whether it's solid or needs renegotiating.

What is the CarHandled Toolkit?

It’s a DIY digital product packed with scripts, calculators, and templates to help you beat the dealership at their own game—without hiring anyone. Launching soon.

Do you work with clients across the U.S.?

Yes—we operate nationwide. We’ve helped clients across dozens of states and can coordinate deals locally or out-of-state, depending on where the best value is.

Are you affiliated with any dealerships?

No. While we have strong industry relationships, our loyalty is to you. We work for the buyer—always.

What if I’m not satisfied with the service?

If you fully engage in the process and feel we didn’t deliver value, let us know. We’ll review your experience and make it right. No stress, no drama.

How do I get started?

Just fill out our quick intake form. It takes 2 minutes, and we’ll respond within 24 hours with next steps.
