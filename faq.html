Frequently Asked Questions

What is CarHandled?

CarHandled is a premium auto consulting service offering concierge car buying, deal reviews, and custom vehicle match strategies—designed to save you time, stress, and money.

How does the Concierge service work?

We handle your car search, compare options, negotiate pricing, and line up the deal. You finalize paperwork directly with the dealer. It’s full-service buying, minus the pressure.

What if I already have a quote?

Use our Deal Review service. We'll analyze your quote and tell you if it's competitive—or if better options exist.

Can you help me decide whether to lease or buy?

Absolutely. Our Car Buyer Toolkit (coming soon) includes calculators and worksheets to break down lease vs. buy comparisons. For tailored guidance, our Car Match Strategy service helps you decide based on your budget, lifestyle, and financial goals—then narrows your best-fit options. You get clarity and confidence, no sales pressure.

Is this service available nationwide?

We are based in the U.S. and operate nationally. We’ve helped clients in nearly every state secure better deals and avoid the games.

Is CarHandled affiliated with any dealerships?

We have strong relationships in the auto industry—including at Jaguar Land Rover Main Line and other trusted dealers nationwide. That said, our loyalty is always to our clients. If a partner dealership offers the best deal, it’s a win-win. We leverage our connections to help you buy smarter—without pressure or bias.
