How it works

From intake to signing — we handle it.

1

You apply — we learn your situation

Tell us your budget, timeline, preferences, and what you've seen so far. We'll follow up within one business day.

2

We build your shortlist and target OTDs

Based on your criteria, we identify the right vehicles and the price range you should be paying — not MSRP.

3

We run the email game

We contact multiple dealers, get written OTD breakdowns, compare line by line, and counter until the number is clean.

4

We prep you for the finance office

Before you step in, you'll know exactly which add-ons to accept, which to decline, and what to say if they push.

5

You show up. You sign. You drive.

The deal is done before you arrive. No negotiation at the table. No surprises on the contract.
