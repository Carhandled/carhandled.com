What We Do (and Don’t)

We’re your personal auto advisor — not a broker, dealership, or lender. That means we don’t buy or sell cars. Instead, we guide you through:

  • Personalized strategy and vehicle shortlisting
  • Market research and quote validation
  • Professional negotiation support or full handling
  • Trade-in strategy and delivery coordination

You sign directly with the dealer. You pay them. We’re just here to make sure the deal is dialed in, and that you win.
