How to request a refund

The process is straightforward. Most refunds are processed within 3–5 business days.

1

Email us at info@carhandled.com

Subject line: "Refund request" works fine. Don't worry about formal language.

2

Include your order email and approximate purchase date

This helps us locate your transaction. If you have your Stripe receipt, forwarding it works perfectly.

3

We process within 3–5 business days

Refunds return to the original payment method. Bank processing times vary — typically 5–10 business days to appear on your statement.

4

You'll get a confirmation email

Once the refund is issued from our end, we'll send you a confirmation. If you don't see it within 7 business days, reply to your original email and we'll investigate.

What's not covered

For full transparency, here's what falls outside our refund policy:

  • Concierge fees once dealer outreach has begun on your behalf (work is in progress)
  • Third-party costs you pay directly (vehicle shipping, DMV, title, taxes, dealer fees) — these are paid to those providers, not to CarHandled
  • Refund requests submitted more than 30 days after toolkit purchase
  • Refund requests for the Concierge after a vehicle has been delivered

If your situation isn't clear-cut, just email us — we'd rather discuss your specific case than let policy fine print get in the way.

Chargebacks & disputes

Before filing a chargeback or dispute with your bank, please email us first. Almost every issue can be resolved directly within a few hours. Chargebacks often take 30–60 days to resolve through banks and can be frustrating for everyone — especially when a quick reply would have solved the problem.

If you've emailed us and we haven't responded within 5 business days, escalation is reasonable. Until then, give us a chance to make it right. We respond fast.
