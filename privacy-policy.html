1. What we collect

Information you give us

  • Contact info — name and email address when you sign up for our free guide, purchase the Toolkit, or apply for Concierge service
  • Purchase info — billing details and transaction records when you buy the Toolkit (handled by Stripe; we never see your full card number)
  • Concierge intake details — vehicle preferences, budget, timeline, and other information you share when applying for our Concierge service
  • Email correspondence — any messages you send us at info@carhandled.com

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data — pages visited, time on site, referring URL, device type, browser, and approximate location (city/country level)
  • Behavioral data — clicks, scrolls, and form interactions captured by our analytics and session recording tools (see Section 3 for full disclosure)
  • Cookies — small data files stored in your browser (see Section 4)

2. How we use it

We use your information for these specific purposes:

  • Deliver what you bought — sending the Toolkit files, processing your Concierge intake, fulfilling your order
  • Communicate with you — order confirmations, follow-up emails, responses to your questions, and educational content if you've subscribed to our list
  • Improve the site and product — understanding which guides are read, where users drop off, and what to fix
  • Provide customer support — looking up your order, processing refunds, helping you access your files
  • Comply with legal obligations — fraud prevention, tax records, and responding to lawful requests

We do not use your data for advertising targeting, lead resale, or any purpose beyond running the business you signed up for.

4. Cookies & tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for two purposes:

Essential cookies

Required for the site to function — keeping your shopping cart, remembering form inputs as you fill them out, and connecting your Stripe checkout to your purchase. These cannot be disabled without breaking site functionality.

Analytics cookies

Set by Google Analytics 4 and Hotjar. These tell us how the site is used in aggregate — which pages are read, where users click, where they drop off. We use this data to improve the experience.

Opting out: You can disable cookies in your browser settings, or use the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on and the Hotjar opt-out tool. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

5. Your rights

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@carhandled.com with the subject line "Privacy Request." We respond within 30 days.

6. Data retention

We keep your data only as long as needed to provide the service or comply with legal obligations:

  • Email subscribers — until you unsubscribe (one click in any email)
  • Toolkit purchases — kept indefinitely for support and refund eligibility, but you can request deletion at any time
  • Concierge clients — kept for 7 years to comply with US tax and business record requirements
  • Analytics data — Google Analytics retains aggregate data for 14 months by default; Hotjar retains session recordings for 365 days

7. Children's privacy

CarHandled is for adults purchasing vehicles. We don't knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, email us at info@carhandled.com and we'll delete it immediately.

8. Policy updates

We update this policy when our tools or practices change. The "Effective" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. For significant changes, we'll notify subscribers by email. Your continued use of the site after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
