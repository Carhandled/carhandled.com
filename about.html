Our Story

I came to the United States with ambition and excitement — but like many, I quickly found myself needing a car. The process was confusing, the dealers were unhelpful, and I had no idea how anything worked. I finally found a car I liked and could afford — but I was taken advantage of. They charged me 18% interest just because I was new to the country and didn’t know better.

I remember sitting in that finance office, heart pounding, signing papers I didn’t fully understand. I walked out with a car — and a pit in my stomach. Later, when I learned how badly I’d been overcharged, I was furious. But mostly, I was disappointed. No one had my back.

A few years later, I got a chance to work inside a dealership. I wanted to give customers the experience I never got — honest, clear, and on their side. And for a while, I did. I helped people save thousands, avoided shady add-ons, and gave them clarity dealers didn’t offer. But the more I tried, the more I ran into resistance. Management didn’t like me advocating too hard for the client. Eventually, I had to walk away.

But I never let go of that mission — to help first-time buyers and anyone walking into a dealership without backup. That’s why we built CarHandled. To protect people like me. To make sure no one signs a deal they’ll regret later. To bring clarity and strategy to a process that’s stacked against the buyer.

Today, we combine real dealership insight, powerful buyer tools, and personal support to help you drive away smarter — and prouder. Whether you use our Toolkit or hire us as your Concierge, the goal is the same: give you the upper hand and peace of mind.

We’ve been on both sides of the table. Now we’re on yours.
