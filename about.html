The 2-minute story

I signed at 18% APR.
Because nobody told me better.

I needed a car in a new country and walked out of a finance office with a payment I didn't fully understand. 18% APR. Junk add-ons. A deal that made sense only to the dealer.

That feeling stuck. I promised myself I wouldn't let it happen to other people.

Years later, working inside dealerships, I learned the full playbook — the OTD email method, which add-ons are pure margin, how to use a pre-approval as leverage. I watched how differently things went when buyers came in prepared.

Read the full story

Inside the dealership, I learned what actually moved deals: clean out-the-door quotes in writing, disciplined counter-offers, and not letting the finance office undo a good negotiation at the last step.

When buyers had the right information and a plan, everything changed. They got better deals, felt in control, and left without regret. That gap — between what dealers know and what buyers know — is why CarHandled exists.

CarHandled is the result: simple tools and clear steps to get a real number in writing, compare options apples-to-apples, and keep control all the way through signing. If you want someone to run the whole play for you, the Concierge does that too.

What we stand for

Three principles. No exceptions.

⚖️

Clean numbers or no deal

We chase written out-the-door quotes and compare like-for-like. No mystery fees. No blended payments hiding the real price.

✉️

Email first, pressure last

Control the process by inbox. Walk in to sign — not to be sold. Dealers negotiate differently in writing than in person.

◎

Simple beats clever

Plain-English scripts and calculators. If it's confusing, it's not helping. The best deal is one you fully understand before you sign.
