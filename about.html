Our 2-minute story

I needed a car in a new country and signed a deal at 18% APR because I didn’t know better. Years later inside a dealership, I learned the playbook—and realized how many people were getting treated like I was. CarHandled exists to flip the script.

I walked into a finance office confused and walked out with a payment I didn’t fully understand. That feeling stuck. I promised I wouldn’t let it happen to other people.

Inside the dealership, I learned what mattered: clean out-the-door quotes, disciplined negotiation, and avoiding junk add-ons. When buyers had the right info and a plan, everything changed.

CarHandled is the result—simple tools and clear steps to get a real number in writing, compare options apples-to-apples, and keep control through signing. If you want someone to run the whole play for you, our Concierge does that too.
